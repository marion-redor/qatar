Medical Wallet

What is medical wallet ?

MedicallWallet is a startup project in the medical sector, created with the aim of making our personal medical data accessible on your smartphone. MedicalWallet is a solution that record and manages the personal medical data in order to simplify the follow-up and check-up of our heath conditions regardless of the doctor or the place of consultation.

How does Medical Wallet work ?

Medical wallet allows you to centralize your medical records in one place, which you will always have with you: your smartphone. This allows you, among other things, to have an overview of your past pathologies, reminders for your medical appointments, medication intake and many other functions.

Why use MedicalWallet ?

Patients can access their health records anywhere they go. Medical Wallet help people especially patient with chronic disease or travellers to have a digital record that they can use in case of having health problem or managing their medication and appointments.
